Common questions

Is there a free trial?

We offer a guided demo using your own data so you can see exactly how Pro-curo works for your use case. Get in touch and we'll arrange one.

What's included in the price?

All core modules — samples, projects, locations, reports, search, and the dashboard — come as standard. Add-on modules are available for specialist needs.

How does support work?

Every licence includes email and phone support during UK business hours. We also provide onboarding sessions and documentation to get your team up and running quickly.

Can I switch from cloud to on-premise?

Absolutely. Pro-curo is designed to run in either environment, and we can help you migrate between them at any time.
